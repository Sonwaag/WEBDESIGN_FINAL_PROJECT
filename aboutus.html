Our Crew

We at OXYCAR are a group of inexperienced young men in a newly formed company. We want to participate in the process of saving our planet. Our goal is to reduce Greenhouse gases in the atmosphere by reducing the amount produced by cars burning fossile fuels. According to NASA “Humans have increased atmospheric CO2 concentration by 47% since the Industrial Revolution began. This is the most important long-lived "forcing" of climate change.” (climate.nasa.gov) The carbon dioxide in the atmosphere has been increasing at an alarming rate the last decades, and we at OXYCAR want to see an end to this problem. Our vision is a future where renewable energy has replaced fossile fuels completely. However we fear this process will take years, maybe decades. Therefore our mission is to enhance the systems we are already using.
