Why is it important to "Take urgent action to combat climate change and its impacts"?

Taken from Science.org: "Climate change is a change in the pattern of weather, and related changes in oceans, land surfaces and ice sheets, occurring over time scales of decades or longer". Also, according to The United Nations website: "Climate change is affecting every country on every continent. It is disrupting national economies and affecting lives. Weather patterns are changing, sea levels are rising, and weather events are becoming more extreme". Even animal species are dying out, because of the changes on out planet. If we don't, the sea levels will raise, entire animal species will die out, the climate will get warmer and more extreme, and it will be irreversible.

Even though we have to stop using these types of transportation and creation, we are already way too dependent to "just" stop. Therefore people must come together and find alternatives to change our ways of living and using.
